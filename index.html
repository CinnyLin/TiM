I took a psychology class in school and I think the ability to analyze my justifications when I waste my time is just the coolest. I believe that data visualization skills can be useful to anyone! Features

Personal Finance

Learn personal finance management strategies, such as budgeting, listing, and long/short-term planning etc, and how to appply them on time management. You will be able to to deal with the to-dos floating in your head, and get the most done with least time. We will be using Google Calendar for implementation.

Psychology

Learn psychological topics such as learning, thinking, motivation and cognition on an introductory level. You will be able to cope with stress, motivate yourself to get work done, and identify common fallacies that hinder your effectiveness.

Data Science

Learn to clean raw calendar data and make visualizations. You will be able to think like a data scientist: determine the plot best and making inferences from those charts as well as recognizing assumptions made to make those conclusions. Prior knowledge in Python is required.
